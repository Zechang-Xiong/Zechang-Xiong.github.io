About Me

Research interests and profile summary

I am a master's student focusing on multimodal affective computing and robust machine learning. My work studies how language, visual, and acoustic signals can be fused to model human emotion and sentiment more reliably.

I am especially interested in uncertainty-aware learning, evidential reasoning, and graph-inspired structures for multimodal understanding. This site foregrounds research communication rather than a long academic CV.
